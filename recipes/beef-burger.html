Beef Burger Recipes

Beef Burger with soya sauce

In this section we will learn to create a beef burger in just 10 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves, sliced tomato, sliced onion, and condiments of your choice (e.g. ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, cheese, pickles, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or a grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, and basil. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and form each into a patty.
  3. Place the patties on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until fully cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for 1-2 minutes.
  5. To assemble the burgers, spread your desired condiments on the bottom buns. Top each bottom bun with a lettuce leaf, a slice of tomato, a slice of onion, and a beef patty. Finish with the top buns and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious beef burger!

Mutton Pizza recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked, chopped mutton
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll the pizza dough out into a large, thin circle and place it on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving about 1/2 inch border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.
  5. Scatter the mutton, onion, green bell pepper, and black olives over the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle the oregano and basil over the toppings.
  7. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your delicious mutton pizza!
